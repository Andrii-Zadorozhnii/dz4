Chalcolestes viridis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chalcolestes viridis, formerly Lestes viridis,[2] is a damselfly of the family Lestidae.[3] It has a metallic green body[4] and at rest it holds its wings away from its body. Its common name is the willow emerald damselfly,[5] the green emerald damselfly,[1] or the western willow spreadwing.[6] It has an elongated abdomen and pale brown spots on its wings and resides in areas of still water with overhanging trees.
